OSMC (https://osmc.tv)
-------

OSMC (Open Source Media Center) is a free and open source social media player founded in 2014 that lets you play back media from your local network, attached storage and the Internet. OSMC is the leading media center in terms of feature set and community and is based on the Kodi project.

-- Free and open source
-- Only takes five minutes to install
-- Has automatic updates and an app store
-- Makes sharing content easy
-- Looks great
